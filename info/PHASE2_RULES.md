# Phase 2 Rules

## Institution and Class Structure
- Institution (College) > Class Group
- Educators belong to a single college by default.
- Groups are scoped to a college.

## Scoring Rules
- Base score per question: 10
- Hint penalty per hint: 2
- Passing score default: 70%

## Hint Quality Expectations
- Hint 1: broad, directional
- Hint 2: more specific, narrows choices
- Hint 3: highly specific, avoids giving the full answer

## Game Limits
- Max questions per game: 30
- Max hints per question: 3

## Sharing Rules
- Educators can duplicate their own games.
- Templates are managed by admins only.
